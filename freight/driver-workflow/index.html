    Driver Workflow & Mobile Access¶

    How drivers receive orders, update container status, collect signatures, and complete deliveries from their phone.

    Overview¶

    Drivers access UTX Freight through their mobile browser — no app download required. Once a dispatcher assigns an order and sends the Magic Driver Link, the driver can:

    • View all assigned orders for the day
    • Update container number and container status
    • Collect signatures for delivery and pickup
    • Mark orders as delivered or returned

    Quick Start¶

    1. The dispatcher sends you a Magic Driver Link via text or email.
    2. Click the link — you are automatically logged in.
    3. Your assigned orders appear on screen.
    4. Tap an order to update its status or collect a signature.

    Logging In¶

    Drivers log in using the Magic Driver Link — a special URL that auto-authenticates without needing a username or password.

    How it works:

    1. A dispatcher assigns you to an order on the Dispatch board.
    2. A share icon appears next to your name.
    3. The dispatcher clicks the icon, copies the link, and sends it to you.
    4. You click the link on your phone and you're in.

    Tip

    Bookmark the link — it stays valid for your session. If it expires, ask your dispatcher to send a new one.

    Viewing Your Orders¶

    Once logged in, you see all orders assigned to you. Each order shows:

    • Order type and order number
    • Customer name and delivery address
    • Container # and container size
    • Special Note — instructions from the dispatcher (shown in red)

    Collecting Signatures¶

    The signature workflow depends on the order type:

    Import Orders (Container Delivery)¶

    Step Action Button
    1 Deliver container to customer Sign DELIVERY
    2 Return empty container to terminal Drop Completed

    Export Orders (Container Pickup)¶

    Step Action Button
    1 Drop empty container at customer Sign DROP
    2 Pick up loaded container Sign PICKUP

    Dry Van / LCL Orders¶

    Step Action Button
    1 Pick up goods Sign PICKUP
    2 Deliver goods Sign DELIVERY

    Transfer Orders¶

    Step Action Button
    1 Move container between terminals Transfer Completed

    Entering the container number

    For Transfer orders, you will be asked to enter the Container Number before completing the transfer.

    How to Sign¶

    1. Tap the signature button (Sign DELIVERY, Sign PICKUP, etc.).
    2. The signature pad appears on screen.
    3. Have the receiver sign with their finger on the pad.
    4. Tap Save to confirm.

    The signature, along with the date and time, is recorded on the order and visible to dispatchers and managers.

    Updating Container Status¶

    Drivers can update the container status from mobile as the delivery progresses:

    • Dropped at Customer — container left at the customer site
    • Empty at Customer — container unloaded
    • Full at Customer — container loaded and ready
    • Returned to terminal — empty container returned

    The status update is immediately visible to dispatchers on the Dispatch board.

    Refreshing Orders¶

    Tap the Refresh button at the bottom of the screen to reload your order list. New assignments from the dispatcher will appear after a refresh.

    FAQ / Troubleshooting¶

    Magic Link doesn't work or shows an error¶

    Solution

    The link may have expired. Ask your dispatcher to generate a new Magic Driver Link from the Dispatch board. Make sure you're opening it in your phone's web browser, not inside an app (like Facebook Messenger's built-in browser).

    Orders are missing from my list¶

    Solution

    Only Dispatched orders assigned to you appear. If the dispatcher just added a new order, tap Refresh at the bottom of the screen. If it still doesn't show, confirm with your dispatcher that the order is dispatched and assigned to your name.

    Signature doesn't save¶

    Solution

    Make sure you have an internet connection. The signature requires a network connection to upload. If you're in a dead zone, wait until you have signal and try again.
