## Quick steps

This section explains how to get up and running with UTX Freight highlighting the basic needs to create your first **Order.**

### Create your first customer

All the orders in UTX Freight are placed on behalf of a contact $inline[icon,fas fa-user] **Person** from a $inline[icon,fas fa-building] **Customers**  list, therefore the first step is to create the Customer company and invite a contact.

- From the left menu, select $inline[icon,fas fa-building] **Customers**
- Click on the **Add a new customer** button on the top right
- Start by entering the **company name** which is the only required field
- Click on **Save Changes**

### Add a contact person

Once you have your first $inline[icon,fas fa-building] **Customer** created, you need to invite a contact $inline[icon,fas fa-user] **Person** for this Customer. Orders will be associated with those contacts and they will be the ones receiving notifications.

- From the left menu, select $inline[icon,fas fa-building] **Customers**
- Using the top filter, find your Customer by typing the company name
- Once the results show up, click on $inline[icon,fas fa-plus] **Add Person**

### Create your first Order

Once you enter your Customer and a contact Person, you can move ahead and create your first Order.

- From the left menu, select $inline[icon,fas fa-truck] **Orders**
- Click on $inline[icon,fas fa-plus] **Create a new Order**

## Next Steps

Once the information is entered and the order submitted you will be ready to proceed with the next steps.

- $inline[icon,fas fa-clock] **Scheduling an appointment**
- $inline[icon,fas fa-location-arrow] **Tracing the shipment**
- $inline[icon,fas fa-user-clock] **Assigning Drivers**
- $inline[icon,fas fa-truck] **Dispatching the order**
- $inline[icon,fas fa-signature] **Delivering and accepting a signature**
- $inline[icon,fas fa-check-double] **Completing the order**
- $inline[icon,fas fa-file-invoice] **Invoicing**
- $inline[icon,fas fa-dollar-sign] **Getting paid**
